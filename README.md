# 🔐 Cybersecurity Risk Assessment Project

## 📌 Overview

This project demonstrates my ability to identify, assess, and mitigate cybersecurity risks in a small business environment. It applies core concepts of risk management and the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

---

## 🏢 Scenario

BrightMart is a small retail company with 25 employees. The company stores customer data, processes online payments, and relies on employee laptops for daily operations. The organization currently has limited cybersecurity controls in place.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Identify key business assets
* Identify threats and vulnerabilities
* Assess cybersecurity risks based on likelihood and impact
* Recommend appropriate security controls

---

## 🧠 Framework Used

* NIST Cybersecurity Framework (Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover)

---

## 📊 Key Risks Identified

* High risk of customer data breach due to weak authentication
* Exposure to malware due to lack of endpoint protection
* Risk of website downtime due to missing network security controls

---

## 🛠️ Security Recommendations

* Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA)
* Install antivirus and endpoint protection tools
* Deploy firewalls and network monitoring systems
* Conduct regular employee cybersecurity awareness training
